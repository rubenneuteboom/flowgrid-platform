1. Introduction

Welcome to Flowgrid Platform ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal data. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Agent Design Wizard and related services.

Summary: We collect only what we need to provide our services. We don't sell your data. Your agent designs and capability models remain yours.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Account Information: Email address, name, organization name, role
  • Wizard Input: Text descriptions, uploaded images (capability diagrams), custom prompts
  • Agent Configurations: Agent names, descriptions, capabilities, process flows

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, screen resolution
  • Session Data: Wizard progress saved locally in your browser

2.3 AI Processing

To provide our AI-powered features, we send data to third-party AI providers:

Feature Provider Data Sent
Image Analysis OpenAI (GPT-4 Vision) Uploaded images
Text Analysis Anthropic (Claude) Text descriptions
Agent Generation Anthropic (Claude) Capability data

These providers process your data according to their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing OpenAI's Privacy Policy and Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the Flowgrid Platform services
  • To generate AI-powered agent designs based on your input
  • To save your wizard progress so you can resume later
  • To communicate with you about your account and services
  • To ensure security and prevent fraud
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage

4.1 Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to save wizard progress. This data:

  • Is stored only on your device
  • Expires after 24 hours of inactivity
  • Can be cleared at any time via your browser settings
  • Is encrypted before storage for additional security

4.2 Server Storage

When you apply/import agents, data is stored in our secure databases:

  • Hosted on Microsoft Azure (West Europe region)
  • Encrypted at rest (AES-256)
  • Encrypted in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • Isolated by tenant (multi-tenant architecture)

5. Data Retention

Data Type Retention Period
Wizard Sessions (local) 24 hours
Wizard Sessions (server) 30 days after completion
Agent Configurations Until you delete them
Account Data Until account deletion + 90 days
Audit Logs 1 year

6. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing
  • Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@flowgrid.io.

7. Cookies and Tracking

8. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • TLS 1.2+ encryption for all data in transit
  • AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • JWT-based authentication with short-lived tokens
  • CSRF protection on all state-changing requests
  • Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
  • Rate limiting to prevent abuse
  • Multi-factor authentication (optional)

9. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • OpenAI: Image analysis (GPT-4 Vision)
  • Anthropic: Text analysis and generation (Claude)
  • Microsoft Azure: Hosting and infrastructure
  • Google Fonts: Typography

10. Children's Privacy

Flowgrid Platform is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or a prominent notice on our platform. Your continued use after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.
